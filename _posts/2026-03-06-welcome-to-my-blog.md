Welcome to my research blog.

I use this space to share brief updates on my work in cancer genomics, single-cell and spatial biology, and AI-based world models for tumor evolution and metastasis.

My research focuses on how genomic alterations reshape regulatory programs, how early events bias metastatic trajectories, and how uncertainty-aware computational models can improve biological discovery and translation.

Here I will post:
- research updates,
- method development notes,
- publication highlights,
- grant and project ideas,
- and perspectives on computational oncology.

Thank you for visiting.
